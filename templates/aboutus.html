Welcome to Track Your Health

Health care Needs to Our Patients

This application aims to help patients, especially those suffering from chronic diseases such as diabetes and high blood pressure. The site provides the ability to track tests as well as include changes in your health so that you are aware of changes in your body. One of the best services offered is a direct contact between the patient and the nurse and the doctor. Thanks to this directly the doctor is aware of changes in the patient and the nurse is also able to help.
